🔬 Methodology

Data Sources

• Spot Orderbooks: Binance (BTCUSDT, BTCUSDC, BTCFDUSD, BTCEUR, BTCUSD), Kraken (BTCUSD, BTCEUR), Coinbase (BTCUSD)

• Futures: Binance BTCUSDT & SOLUSDT perpetual futures

• AggTrades: High-frequency trade aggregation data for validation

Analysis Window

• Period: 2026-02-24 00:00 UTC to 2026-02-26 22:00 UTC (~70 hours)

• Sampling: 1-second intervals with gap-free continuous data

• Lag Range: -60s to +60s tested for optimal cross-correlation

Cross-Correlation Method

• Pearson correlation coefficient calculated at each lag offset

• Positive lag indicates first series leads second series

• Statistical significance tested with p-values < 0.05
